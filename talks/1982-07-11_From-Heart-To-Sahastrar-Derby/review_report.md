# Language Review – From-Heart-To-Sahastrar-Derby, 1982-07-11

## Process

Review of `transcript_uk.txt` (full paragraphed Ukrainian text) using 2 parallel
reviewers + 1 critic filter, per `templates/language_review_template.md`.
**This is the third review pass**; pass 1 (5 corrections) and pass 2
(11 corrections) were already applied. Pass 2 established the rule
interpretation that uppercase 1st-person pronouns (Я/Мені/Мій) are reserved
exclusively for Shri Mataji, Incarnations receive only 3rd-person uppercase
(Він/Його/Йому), and relative pronouns are not capitalized — this precedent
was honored by the Critic in this pass.

Paragraph numbers (¶) refer to line numbers in `transcript_uk.txt`
(lines 1–4 are the header; body paragraphs are lines 6–91).

Mechanical checks performed: no Latin/Cyrillic mixing, no `„"`/`""` quotes,
no double spaces, guillemets balanced (141/141), em-dash ` – ` consistent.
Apostrophe audit: all 62 apostrophes were straight `'` (U+0027), while
`glossary/CLAUDE.md` mandates `’` (U+2019) — flagged as L39.
