against the English original, `glossary/CLAUDE.md`, `terms_lookup.yaml`, and
`terms_context.yaml`. Reviewers L (Language) and S (SY Domain) ran in parallel; the
Critic filtered their findings before application.

Mechanical pre-checks (scripted): apostrophes uniformly `’` (U+2019, 19×); dashes
are en-dash `–` (U+2013, 54×) plus 13 hyphens all in legitimate compounds

Latin/Cyrillic letter mixing; no double spaces; no space before punctuation;
ellipsis (`Немає...`) is three dots with no preceding space. All `Сахаджа Йоґ-`
forms correctly use ґ (ghe) and are internally consistent (`Йоґи`, `Йоґу`, `Йоґа`,
`Йоґою`, locative `Йозі`).
