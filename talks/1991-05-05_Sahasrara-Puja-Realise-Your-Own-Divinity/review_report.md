# Language Review – Sahasrara-Puja-Realise-Your-Own-Divinity, 1991-05-05

## Process

2+1 review of `transcript_uk.txt` (full paragraphed Ukrainian text) against the
English original `transcript_en.txt`, the glossary (`terms_lookup.yaml`,
`terms_context.yaml`), and the capitalization/orthography rules in
`glossary/CLAUDE.md`. Paragraph numbers refer to line numbers in
`transcript_uk.txt`.

Reviewer L (Language) and Reviewer S (SY Domain) ran in parallel and produced
candidate-correction tables. The Critic then filtered both tables, removing
false positives, source-faithful renderings, and trivial style preferences.

This is a **second review pass**; it supersedes the earlier report at this
path. The earlier pass raised 6 candidates and approved 0. Its items were
independently re-raised or re-examined here (VIBGYOR, «Всепроникаюча»,
the tiger comma-dash, «Сахадж Йоґу», «осідок Серця», sentence-initial «Мені»)
and its "Remove" verdicts were **upheld** — those renderings remain unchanged.
This pass additionally found five grammar/punctuation defects the earlier pass
missed; all five were approved and applied.

Mechanical pre-checks (scripted): no double spaces, no space before
punctuation, no straight `"`/`'` quotes or apostrophes, no `„“”` quotes, no
hyphen-as-dash, no double periods, no Latin/Cyrillic mixing inside words. The
only Latin token is the acronym `VIBGYOR` (¶9), glossed inline as
«семи кольорів». The ellipsis in ¶35 («відгуків... вони») mirrors the English
source ellipsis and follows the no-space-before rule.

## Results

### L. Language (Orthography + Grammar + Punctuation)

| # | Paragraph | Error | Context | Fix |
|---|-----------|-------|---------|-----|
| L1 | 15 | Predicate agreement: quantifier «небагато» + gen. pl. of an inanimate noun takes a singular predicate | «лише дуже небагато ниток Кундаліні **утримуються** в спинному мозку» | «…утримується в спинному мозку» |
| L2 | 19 | Predicate agreement: «більшість» + gen. pl. with the predicate immediately following the subject takes the singular | «Та все ж більшість людей **розпізнають** це на ментальному рівні.» | «…більшість людей розпізнає це…» |
| L3 | 27 | Parenthetical «можна сказати» must be comma-delimited on **both** sides (cf. the correctly punctuated «…, можна сказати, так:» earlier in the same paragraph) | «**Або можна сказати,** у центральну нервову систему вбудований страх.» | «Або, можна сказати, у центральну нервову систему вбудований страх.» |
| L4 | 31 | Mixed indirect/direct speech: conjunction «що» + colon before a direct quote is non-standard; direct speech is introduced by the verb + colon | «І розумом ви знаєте**, що:** «Так, так, це працює», – скажете ви» | «І розумом ви знаєте: «Так, так, це працює», – скажете ви» |
| L5 | 45 | Same «що:» + direct-quote error as L4 | «…це абсолютно вирішити в собі сьогодні**, що:** «Ми збираємося здійснити це…»» | «…вирішити в собі сьогодні: «Ми збираємося здійснити це…»» |
| L6 | 41 | Colon followed by a subordinate «що»-clause | «Який прекрасний зв’язок створено**: що** цей центр тут є осідком серця» | Drop «що» after the colon? |
| L7 | 39 | Reference agreement: «Воно починає литися» after plural «всі благословення» | «…ви отримуєте всі благословення. **Воно** починає литися на вас…» | «Вони починають литися…»? |

Other mechanical categories (apostrophe `’`, quotes `«»` at all levels,
en-dash ` – ` spacing, ellipsis form, gender/case agreement elsewhere, verb
conjugations) — **no defects**.

### Critic Filter

| Source | # | Verdict | Reason |
|--------|---|---------|--------|
| L | L1 | **Keep** | Clear grammar norm: «небагато» + inanimate gen. pl. requires a singular predicate («утримується»). |
| L | L2 | **Keep** | Predicate immediately follows «більшість людей» — the standard written norm requires the singular; same category as L1, applied consistently. |
| L | L3 | **Keep** | Genuine punctuation rule (вставне словосполучення відокремлюється з обох боків); the identical construction is already punctuated correctly earlier in the same paragraph, so this also fixes an intra-paragraph inconsistency. |
| L | L4 | **Keep** | Genuine error: «що» + colon mixes indirect- and direct-speech marking. Minimal fix — drop «, що», keep the colon. |
| L | L5 | **Keep** | Same error and same fix as L4, applied for consistency. |
| L | L6 | **Remove** | Mirrors the English source structure («What a beautiful connection made: that this centre…»); acceptable in the spoken-transcript register. Not a genuine error. |
| L | L7 | **Remove** | False positive. «Воно» mirrors the vague English «It starts pouring on you», and «благословення» is formally identical in neuter singular and plural, so the collective-singular reading is defensible. |
| S | S1 | **Remove** | Source-faithful: the English itself has capital «the Heart» in ¶34 and lowercase «heart» in ¶41, so the translation's variation reproduces the source exactly. (Concurs with the previous review pass, which reached the same verdict on this item.) |
| S | S2 | **Remove** | VIBGYOR is the standard rainbow-spectrum mnemonic used verbatim in the source; it has no established Cyrillic equivalent and the translation already adds the gloss «семи кольорів». (Concurs with the previous pass.) |
| S | S3 | **Remove** | The English deliberately switches «Sahaja Yoga» → «Sahaj Yog» in the same sentence, and `terms_context.yaml` states the «сахаджа»/«сахадж» forms are interchangeable, per original. (Concurs with the previous pass.) |

## Summary

- Language (L): 7 issues found, 5 approved by Critic
- SY Domain (S): 3 issues found, 0 approved by Critic
- Total corrections applied: **5**

The translation is of high quality: deity-pronoun capitalization, glossary
terminology, transliteration, orthography (apostrophes, «» quotes, ` – `
en-dashes, ellipsis), and SY-term consistency are all correct. The five
applied fixes are small but genuine grammar/punctuation defects (two
quantifier-agreement errors, one missing parenthetical comma, and two
«що:»-before-direct-speech constructions). All terminology-level candidates
were rejected as faithful renderings of the English source, in agreement with
the previous review pass.
