| S1 | «акаша»→«Акаша» | **Remove** | Internally consistent. Corpus also uses lowercase акаша/акашу/акаші for the element. Forcing a capital would break parallelism with the sibling elements. |
| S2 | «груповщина» | **Remove** | Understood, negatively-connoted equivalent of «groupism»; the calque is intelligible and replacing it is a style choice, not a correction. Already adjudicated and removed in the 2026-05-30 review — concur. |
