The translation is of high quality. Glossary discipline, transliteration
(Аґія, Ґанеша/Ґанеші/Ґанешею, Сахаджа Йозі), deity-pronoun capitalization,
quotation-mark style («» at all levels), em-dash usage (` – `), ellipsis
(`...`), and language-name lowercasing are all consistent with the canonical
rules. The genuine issues of this pass were mechanical or grammatical: the
straight-apostrophe glyph throughout the file, two missing junction commas,
one title-capitalization slip, and a handful of localized grammar defects
(agreement, verb government, collocation, relative-clause placement) — all
fixed with minimal edits that preserve the translator's voice and the source's
spoken-lecture register.
