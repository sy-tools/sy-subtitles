mirrored in UK): paragraphs 43–66 (clean) and 67–185 (line-wrapped) are the same
translation; 186–299 is a second, more literal translation. Variations that
faithfully mirror differing source spellings (e.g. Shivji/Shivaji,

source-faithful, not errors. Corrections were applied to *all* parallel copies of
an affected sentence. Paragraph numbers below are line numbers of
`transcript_uk.txt`.

Mechanical pre-checks (scripted): no Latin characters mixed into Cyrillic words;
no straight quotes/apostrophes; quotation marks «» only; dash consistently
` – ` (U+2013 with spaces); apostrophe U+2019; no double spaces; ellipsis `...`
with no space before it.
