# Language Review – 1991-02-16_Mahashivaratri-Puja-Four-Nadis-of-the-Heart, 2026-07-17

## Process

Reviewed `transcript_uk.txt` (101 lines / 100 paragraphs) against the English
original (`transcript_en.txt`), `glossary/CLAUDE.md`, `glossary/terms_lookup.yaml`
and `glossary/terms_context.yaml`, using 2 parallel reviewers (L + S) + 1 Critic filter.
Paragraph numbers (§) refer to line numbers in `transcript_uk.txt`.

This is a second review pass (first pass: 2026-05-31, one correction applied —
«Істина» §94). The translation is of high quality overall: deity-pronoun
capitalization, glossary transliteration (Шіва, Садашіва, Аґія, Вішуддхі,
Муладхара, Сахасрара, Брахмадева, Йоґешвара, Махамайя), `сахаджа йоґ`
declensions, «» quotation marks (including nested levels) and ` – ` dashes are
applied correctly and consistently. Character-level scans found no straight
apostrophes, no `„"/""` quotes, no double spaces, no space-before-punctuation,
no Latin/Cyrillic mixing inside words (the romanized shloka in §15 is an
intentional quotation). This pass focused on grammar (verb government, negative
polarity), direct-speech capitalization, and orthography of the venue name,
which the first pass had not covered.
