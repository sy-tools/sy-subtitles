### Critic Filter

| Source | # | Verdict | Reason |
|--------|---|---------|--------|
| L | L1 | **Keep** | Rule-backed: repeated conjunction «чи…чи» requires the comma before the second «чи». Corpus confirms the convention: «чи X, чи щось» ≈ 9 occurrences vs 3 without comma. *Overrides the 2026-05-29 Critic's "optional" verdict on the same span — the pravopys rule plus corpus dominance outweigh loose-tail usage.* |
| L | L2 | **Keep** | Genuine grammar error that distorts meaning: the participle attributes the giving of Realization to the listeners. Minimal swap «давши» → «отримавши» keeps the main clause intact and the meaning faithful. |
| L | L3 | **Keep** | Attributive «покінчене (життя)» is non-normative; the impersonal «з … покінчено» is the norm and the corpus idiom (17 occurrences of impersonal «покінчено» across talks; attributive form virtually absent). *Overrides the 2026-05-29 Critic's rejection of «має бути завершене»: this round's fix uses the standard impersonal construction instead of a synonym swap, so it is a rule fix, not a stylistic rewrite.* |
| L | L4 | **Remove** | «те-то» is a colloquial particle rendering the loose spoken register of the source («I am doing this» inside a rambling complaint); not an unambiguous error — replacing it is a style preference. |
| L | L5 | **Keep** | Current text asserts near-opposite modality («must not yet be born») vs the intended «has not yet been reborn»; the following clauses («у неї виникає проблема, але вона цього не відчуває») confirm the intended reading. |
| L | L6 | **Keep** | EN has two distinct words («this, that»); the verbatim doubling «тим, тим» is an obvious typo. «цим, тим» is the minimal faithful pair. |
| L | L7 | **Keep** | «дехто» takes singular agreement (cf. correct «дехто з вас… сприймає» in §15 of this very talk and corpus-wide «дехто з них думає»); «деякі з них були друзями» preserves the plural predicate and matches EN. |
| L | L8 | **Keep** | Conjunction junction «що» + «коли» without correlative «то» requires the comma; cf. the correctly punctuated «що, якщо треба звести цей будинок…» in §44 of the same text. |
| S | S1 | **Remove** | The source «sympathetic bondage» is itself ambiguous; «симпатичне» preserves that ambiguity. Not a glossary term — any replacement would be interpretation, not correction. (Same verdict as the 2026-05-29 round.) |
| S | S2 | **Remove** | Sahaja case is explicitly translator's discretion per `terms_context.yaml` («регістр — на розсуд перекладача / за оригіналом»); lowercase in predicative position is a defensible, consistent choice. |
| S | S3 | **Remove** | Same discretion rule; lowercase here supports the deliberate contrast with «асахаджа» (always lowercase) within one sentence. |
